About Us

A native New Yorker raised in the Bronx, and the daughter of immigrant parents from international backgrounds, Dealal Ahmed is a graduate of Murry Bergtraum High School, a school specializing in Business. She obtained her Bachelors of Arts in Political Science from Fordham University in 2010 and her Juris Doctor from Pace University School of Law in 2013. As a student, Dealal Ahmed also worked in house for an Engineering firm in 2012. In 2011, Ms. Ahmed interned for an international law firm in Cairo, Egypt and a personal injury firm in New York City.
